Thank you for being a beacon of hope! Your kindness is reaching orphaned children, youths, and families in need, bringing them love, support, and a brighter future. We couldn’t do this without you!

Support Us

We accept fairly used and new children’s clothing, shoes and back packs which can be dropped off at our annual donation drive event. Date and time of each event will be posted on the event page.

Please wash your donated clothing in cold water, fold them, separate them by size and gender. Next, place your donations in clear plastic bins. You can often find them at very affordable prices at many department stores. Otherwise, plastic lawn-and-garden bags, which are thicker and more durable than standard trash bags, are the next best alternative

​Finally, bring your donations to the venue of our donation drive. Your donated items will be sorted and sent directly to kids who have none.

Project Clothe A Child is a 501(c)(3) organization. Donations are tax-deductible.
