Our Projects

Our events provide the platforms for achieving our goal of reaching more orphan children, youths and others from low income families.

PROJECT EDUCATE

A scholarship and education assistance initiative created to support economically and socially disadvantaged children under the age of 18.

Donate To Project

PROJECT IMPACT

Project Impact is an annual mentorship seminar organized to educate children and youths on financial literacy and the basics of entrepreneurship.

    Program Goals:
  • (1) Equip underprivileged students (18 and under) who demonstrate high academic achievement with financial resources that will help them apply to college and
  • (2) help students prepare to navigate their finances after high school
  • (3) Educate students on the basics of entrepreneurship to show them a part to becoming self sufficient through entrepreneurship.
